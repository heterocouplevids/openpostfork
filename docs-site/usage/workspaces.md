# Workspaces

Workspaces are the main organizational boundary in OpenPost.

## What belongs to a workspace

- Connected social accounts
- Uploaded media
- Posts and threads
- Prompts and scheduling settings
- Team members and pending invitations

## Why they matter

They let you separate brands, teams, or personal and work publishing without mixing credentials and media libraries.

## Invite team members

Workspace admins can invite collaborators from Settings > Team.

1. Open Settings.
2. Use the Team section to enter the collaborator email and role.
3. Copy the generated invite link and send it to the collaborator.

The invited user must sign in with the invited email address before accepting
the link. Accepted invites add the user to the workspace immediately.

Pending invitations count toward team seats until they are accepted, revoked, or
expire. Revoke unused invites from the Team section when you need to free a
reserved seat.
